Counterpick Coverage

Exploratory

Which additional champion most improves a pool when the opposing laner is known? Candidates are ranked by one decomposable measure.

How this ranking is calculated
opponent weight × max(0, candidate WR − best displayed pool WR)

The contribution is calculated separately for every opponent and summed. Opponent weights come from lane pick rates and are normalized once over the current pool’s fixed, displayed matchup universe. When a pool has multiple champions, its baseline is the best displayed estimate for each opponent.

Candidate additions

Candidate champions ranked by marginal observed counterpick coverage

Population-level data assuming the opponent is known and every pool champion is fully learned. Raw aggregate rates can reflect general champion strength and specialist or off-meta selection as well as matchup complementarity; this is not a personal or causal recommendation.
