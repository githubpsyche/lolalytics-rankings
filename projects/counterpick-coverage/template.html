How this ranking is calculated
opponent weight × max(0, addition WR − current champion WR)

The contribution is calculated separately for every opponent and summed. Opponent weights come from lane pick rates and are normalized once over the current champion’s displayed matchup universe. The second champion contributes only where its displayed estimate is better, because the current champion remains available otherwise.

Population-level data assuming the opponent is known and both selected champions are fully learned. Raw aggregate rates can reflect general champion strength and specialist or off-meta selection as well as matchup complementarity; this is not a personal or causal recommendation.
