How this ranking is calculated
opponent weight × max(0, addition matchup effect − current matchup effect)

A matchup effect is Lolalytics Delta 2 retained in proportion to matchup sample size. Positive means the champion performs better than normalized win rates predict. The current champion remains available wherever the addition is not better.

Choose an addition to inspect its opponent-level calculation.

Population-level data assuming the opponent is known and both selected champions are fully learned. Adjustment addresses matchup sample size, not specialist or off-meta selection, player proficiency, future-patch stability, or causal effects.
