How this ranking is calculated
opponent weight × max(0, adjusted addition WR − adjusted current WR)

The contribution is calculated separately for every opponent and summed. Opponent weights come from lane pick rates and are normalized once over the complete eligible roster. The second champion contributes only where its adjusted estimate is better, because the current champion remains available otherwise.

Choose an addition to inspect its opponent-level calculation.

Population-level data assuming the opponent is known and both selected champions are fully learned. Adjustment addresses matchup sample size, not specialist or off-meta selection, player proficiency, future-patch stability, or causal effects.
